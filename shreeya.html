Shreeya Khurana

Shreeya, a current freshman Computer Science (and potentially AI) major at Carnegie Mellon University, is the President and Founder of MATHgirls. As a huge advocate of Women in STEM, she started planned this program while in 8th grade at Takoma Park Middle School and launched it in her freshman year at Montgomery Blair High School in hopes of being able to encourage and motivate younger underserved and underprivileged girls into STEM fields and increase their confidence, not only in their math skills but also in themselves.

In her free-time, Shreeya enjoys ice skating, listening (and jamming) to music, and participating in outreach and volunteering.
